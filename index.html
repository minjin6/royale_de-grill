About Us

we create delicious memories

Royale De-Grill is UK’s leading Afro-Caribbean flame grill and barbecue culinary restaurant. We specialise in charcoal grill catering ideal for all celebrations. Our authentic dishes of delicious, exceptionally well-prepared food give our customers the food experience they will love and never forget.

Our mission is to bring our customers quality restaurant food with Passion, care and energy. We provide food in a fast, mobile friendly environment that appeal to pride conscious, healthy minded consumers.

Our passion as an afro-Caribbean restaurant is to put a yum on people’s faces, satisfying customers every time they eat our food and doing it better than any other catering service company in the world.
